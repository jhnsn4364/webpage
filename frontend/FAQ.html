FAQ:

What are the facility hours?

The building is open from 8:00am until 8:00pm, and any available rooms can be reserved between those times.

Who can reserve a room?

Anyone with a valid UH ID can reserve a room, including students, teachers, and faculty.

How long can I reserve a room?

Rooms are reserved in 1 hour increments. If you need a room for more than 1 hour, you will need to submit an additional reservation for each individual hour.

Can I reserve a room for my club?

Yes, simply reserve the room under you or another club member's UH ID for the desired time.

How do I cancel a reservation?

Send an email request to our staff at "cajohn32@cougarnet.uh.edu" with the following information:

  • Name
  • UH ID
  • Room number
  • Reservation date
  • Reservation time
