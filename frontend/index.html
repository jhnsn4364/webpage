Sugar Land Student Center

The Sugar Land Student Center is a two-story building that provides a welcoming space for students studying at the Sugar Land Campus to relax, study, and collaborate with other students on campus. Features include a large central seating area with tables and couches for students to use between classes, as well as a number of private rooms that can be reserved for students, faculty, and student-organizations to fit their needs.
