Facility Rules and Regulations:

  • Open to all enrolled students, faculty, and registered guests.
  • Student ID required for entry after 8 PM.
  • Respect others—no disruptive behavior, harassment, or excessive noise.
  • Clean up after yourself; dispose of trash properly.
  • No smoking or vaping anywhere inside the building.
  • Report damaged equipment or unsafe conditions to staff immediately.
  • Maintain a low noise level; designated silent zones must be completely quiet.
  • Reservations required through the student center website or office.
  • Events must end by closing time unless special permission is granted.
  • Follow all emergency procedures and posted evacuation routes.
  • No weapons or hazardous materials allowed.
  • Personal belongings are your responsibility—lost items go to the front desk.
  • Campus security has authority to enforce all rules.
